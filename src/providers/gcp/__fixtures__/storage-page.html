Listing buckets

This page describes how to list the buckets in your project.

Console

  1. Go to the Cloud Storage Buckets page.

Command line

gcloud storage ls
 
buckets = storage_client.list_buckets()
for bucket in buckets:
    print(bucket.name)
 
{"name": "my-bucket"}
Note: You need the storage.buckets.list permission.
Caution: Listing buckets counts against your quota.
Warning: Do not expose the bucket list publicly.
Key Point: Bucket names are globally unique.

Replace BUCKET_NAME with your bucket name.

gsutil ls gs://my-bucket
